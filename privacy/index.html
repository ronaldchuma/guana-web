Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Guana ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Guana mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use Guana, we may collect:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Your profile photo and bio, if you choose to add them.
  • Trip information: Origin, destination, departure time, price, and seat availability for rides you create or join.
  • Messages: Content of messages you send to other users through the app.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for app functionality and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the ride-sharing service
  • Connect drivers and passengers
  • Enable in-app messaging between users
  • Send you service-related notifications (e.g., trip updates, account verification)
  • Improve the app experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information in these cases:

  • With other users: Your display name, profile photo, and trip details are visible to other Guana users to facilitate ride-sharing.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Your data is processed in accordance with their privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure Keychain. We use encrypted storage for sensitive local data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your profile information at any time through the app
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Export your data by contacting us

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@guana.app.
