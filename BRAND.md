# Guana Brand Guide

## Identity

**Name:** Guana
**Domain:** guana.app
**Tagline:** Share rides across Costa Rica
**Positioning:** A lifestyle ride-sharing platform for travelers in Costa Rica — adventure-first, community-driven, trust-focused.

## Tone & Voice

- **Friendly and warm** — not corporate. Feels like a friend who knows the roads.
- **Adventure-oriented** — references waves, hikes, beaches, getaways. Not "commuting."
- **Trust-building** — emphasizes verified profiles, ratings, and community safety.
- **Clear and direct** — short sentences, actionable language. "Find your ride." "Let's go."
- **Bilingual** — English and Spanish (Costa Rica). Same warmth in both.

## Iconography

- System SF Symbols in the iOS app
- Unicode emoji for web (keep it simple)
- No custom icon set yet

## Photography Style

- Coastal Costa Rica landscapes
- Natural light, warm tones
- People in transit / adventure settings
- The app uses a signup hero image of a surfer/beach scene

## Brand Don'ts

- No cold/corporate blues — always warm, muted navy
- No sharp corners — everything is rounded (20px cards, 14px buttons)
- No heavy drop shadows — subtle and airy
- No emoji in formal copy — only in feature highlights
- No uppercase body text — only brand name "GUANA" in nav/headers
